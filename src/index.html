United States Railroad Service

We have been a leader of safety, quality and production within the railroad and crane rail industry since 1999.

We specialize in track inspection, maintenance, safety compliance and construction. We have an abundance of knowledge in FRA, OSHA, MSHA and CPUC.

Our numerous years of experience in LRT, mainlines, short lines, narrow gage, industrial, refineries and military tracks has allowed our company to become a strong asset to all the clientele we serve. Safety and Quality are what we are, Safety and Quality is what we produce.
